This widget is divided into two main sections designed to provide you with an at-a-glance overview of patient data.


1. Age Distribution Line Chart

The upper section displays a line chart illustrating the distribution of your patients across three age categories (0–25 years, 26–55 years, and 56+ years) over the past six months. This visual helps identify trends and changes in patient demographics over time.


2. Patient Status Summary

The lower section presents a status dashboard that highlights three key metrics for the current month:

  • Upcoming Appointments: Number of appointments scheduled.
  • In Progress: Number of appointments currently awaiting your review.
  • Reviewed: Number of appointments you have already reviewed this month.

This widget enables quick monitoring of patient workflow and demographic trends, helping doctors make informed decisions and manage their workload efficiently.
